Pet Statistics

6.5 million companion animals enter animal shelters every year.
Approximately 3.3 million are dogs and 3.2 million are cats.
Approximately 1.5 million shelter animals are euthanized (670,000 dogs and 860,000 cats).
Only 1 out of every 10 dogs born will find a premanent home.
Overpopulation leads to millions of "excess" animals to be killed anually.
Sources:
dosomething.org
ASPCA (America Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Society)
