About Us

Our main idea: to help animals in shelters who don't get adopted.

There are so many animals who don't have homes and who never get adpoted. What we wanted to do is help those animals finally have a forever home.
Our website allows people to browse around different profiles of dogs, cats, bunnies, etc. and let them have insight in what animal the would be adopting.
Shelters would be able to make profiles on this website and so would users around the Bay Area. This would cause les animals on the streets and more in the hands of loving families.

This is only a demo of what we'd like our website. If we ever decide to advance in this website, it will be fully functioning.
